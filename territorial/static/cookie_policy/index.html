Cookie Policy

Last Update: 29 March 2024

1. General Information
FX Client uses cookies to improve the user experience. You can find out more about cookies in the sections below. You don't have to accept cookies in order to use FX Client. We recommend cookies as they will improve your gaming experience. We also use Local Storage to store your win counts and settngs. To understand how we use your information, you may also check out our privacy policy: fxclient.github.io/FXclient/privacy_policy

2. What is a cookie?
Cookies are small text files that are stored on your computer. For example, a cookie can be used to remember your username.

3. What is Local Storage?
Local Storage is a place where we can store some information like your settings. It is not accessible by FX Client servers and is private.

4. What types of cookies do we use?
We use functionality cookies to store your preferences:
your username; a boolean, indicating if your interface is magnified or not; the password of your one-vs-one account; the color of your country; selected emojis;
Furthermore, we use a cookie that assigns a random number to your device.

5. How do our servers handle cookies transmitted by users?
We do not store and process cookies on our servers. Cookies on the client side will be deleted after one year of inactivity. Cookies will not be shared with third parties.
